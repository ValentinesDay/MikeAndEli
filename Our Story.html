first datejune 15
There once was a geeky boy & a slightly nerdy girl each searching for eachother, but never knowing it. One day they connected online & exchanged messages. The day came to meet and the slightly nerdy girl found herself walking into Bad Apple and meeting the geeky boy. They grab a table and talk (well mostly her). They talk about their families and siblings (0 for him & 6 for her), the jobs (software developer for him & recruiting for her) and hobbies (reading for both!) The date is going great and they move to another bar for more drinks and talking. The slightly nerdy girl realizes how late it has become and must leave. She makes a quick goodbye and hopes he'll text her again.

Birthday & First Kissjune 28
That following Friday (the geeky boy's birthday) they find themselves at a restaurant ordering a bottle of wine and eating delicious food. Afterwards they move to a loud bar where their mutual poor hearing forces them to sit closer and close. Every touch is electric! After confirmation that it is no longer his birthday (first kisses shouldn't very be confused with birthday kisses) they exit the bar to go for a night cap and kiss for the first time!
